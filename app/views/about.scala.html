Have you got bored of going out to same old places and doing same old activities in your weekends or other free days? Well, we did, and we also realized that a lot of other people face the same problem. So we decided to take up this challenge, and work out a solution. This led to the creation of Shadence, a platform for discovering experiences in your city.


Our purpose here is to help you make the best use of your free time. To make this possible we are on constant look out for interesting experiences that young working people, such as ourselves, will enjoy after a week of hard work. We invest a huge amount of time and effort looking for unique and interesting experiences in your city, so that you do not have to.


As it requires a lot of hard work and focus to find out quality experiences, for now, we are only live for the great city of London. However, we will be expanding in other cities as well.


We would love to hear from you for any feedback, suggestion or comment from you. So please feel free to write us at info@@shadence.com.
